Who We Are

Creative Trucking & Logging, LLC — rooted in Macon, Georgia.

Our Story

We began as a two-rig hauling crew and grew into a full logging outfit serving farm families, private landowners, and local mills across Middle Georgia. Our goal is simple: leave a tract cleaner, safer, and more valuable for the next rotation.

Every project starts with a walk-through and a plain-English harvest plan. We flag SMZs, verify tract boundaries, and map entrances to minimize disturbance. Technology supports the work: GPS mapping, digital load tickets, and maintenance logs for high uptime.

We merchandize aggressively — separating saw timber, chip-n-saw, and pulpwood to capture value by product class. Day-to-day communication with mills keeps trucks turning and prices current.

Compliance is standard, not a selling point. We follow Georgia BMPs, stabilize water bars, seed exposed soil where needed, and photograph the close-out for your records.

Whether you manage 40 acres or 400, we align the harvest with your objectives: thinning to unlock growth, final harvest to reset a stand, storm cleanup, or roadwork for easier access.

What We Do

Three focused offerings that cover planning, production, and the path to market.

Harvest Planning & Cutting

Selective Thinning and Final Harvests

We design cut plans to unlock growth, improve wind firmness, or safely reset a stand. Feller-bunchers, skidders, and loaders are sized for efficient production with minimal soil impact.

SMZs are flagged; creek crossings are avoided or bridged. Water bars go in before we leave.

You receive a straightforward schedule with daily production reports per product class.

Merchandizing & Logistics

Sorting, Ticketing, and On-Time Hauling

At the deck we separate saw logs, chip-n-saw, and pulpwood; loads are ticketed digitally and tracked by tract. Tight mill coordination keeps turnarounds fast and cash flow steady.

Drivers follow verified routes and scale-house standards. Safety checks and tie-down audits are routine.

We chase blended price per ton — not just more tons — to protect landowner value.

Roadwork & Reforestation

Access, Stabilization, and Replanting Support

We repair entrances, stabilize skid trails, and build water turnouts. Decks are flattened and debris windrowed to speed site prep for replanting.

Need seedlings and a planter? We connect you with trusted nurseries and crews, then coordinate timing.

The aim: leave access better than we found it and the site ready for the next stand.

How We Work

Three simple steps — and you can expect updates at each stage.

1Walk-Through & Plan. We tour the tract, flag SMZs, map access, and align on goals and timing. You receive a concise plan with a schedule.

2Cut, Sort, Haul. Crews produce clean decks; we sort by product class and coordinate daily with mills. Digital tickets track every load.

3Close-Out & Next Steps. Stabilization, photos, and a summary of loads and payments. We connect you with seedling suppliers and planters if replanting.

Walk-through → Production → Close-out

Our Aim

Stewardship that pays — healthy forests and healthy ledgers.

We operate on generational timelines. That means respecting buffers, keeping mud off public roads, documenting the work, and leaving access better than we found it. Healthy stands reach higher-value classes faster when treated right — and that’s good business for everyone.
